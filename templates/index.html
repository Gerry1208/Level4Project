Welcome to Names!

The Names app helps you to learn the names of the students in your tutorial group(s) - accessible here or on the navigation bar above.

You can learn the student names in 3 ways:

  1. Study the list of names and pictures
  2. Test yourself using the Multiple Choice Quiz - a face will be shown to you with a number of names underneath - pick the right one
  3. Test youself using the Self Marking quiz method available - again a face will be shown to you but you only have to think who it is, then reveal the answer to see if you were correct.
    If you got it wrong, take the time to study the name and face, try to learn it for next time.
    Score yourself right or wrong and move onto the next image

Research into learning has shown that testing is better than studying, as the mental act of trying to recall the name helps strengthen pathways in the brain

The Self Marking method has stood out as the best method of learning
